## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take security very seriously. If you have found a security vulnerability in VAULT, we appreciate your help in disclosing it to us in a responsible manner.

**Please DO NOT open an issue to report a security vulnerability.**

Instead, please send an email to **security@b2g-vault.com**. We will acknowledge your email within 48 hours, and we'll send a more detailed response within 48 hours indicating the next steps.

We appreciate your efforts to responsibly disclose your findings, and will make every effort to acknowledge your contributions.
